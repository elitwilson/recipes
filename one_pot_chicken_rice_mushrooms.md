## Instructions

1. **Season and sear** - Season thighs with salt, pepper, smoked paprika. Sear skin-side down in large pot/Dutch oven until golden (5 mins), flip and sear 3 more mins. Remove chicken.
2. **Build umami base** - In same pot with drippings, sautÃ© onion and mushrooms until mushrooms release moisture and start browning (5-6 mins). Add garlic (30 seconds), then tomato paste (1 minute, stirring constantly).
3. **Add rice and liquid** - Stir in rice, then broth and 2 tbsp Worcestershire. Nestle chicken back in, skin-side up.
4. **Simmer** - Bring to boil, reduce to low, cover and cook 18 minutes.
5. **Finish** - Add bell pepper and peas on top, cover 5 more minutes. Taste and add more Worcestershire if desired. Let rest 5 minutes.
